    Before Reading

    What is an orangutan? Where do they live?

    After Reading

    Answer the questions, supporting your responses by citing evidence from the text.

    1. Who is Katty? How did she become an orphan?

    2. Why are farmers burning the rain forest in Indonesia?

    3. What were some of the consequences of last year’s fires?

    4. What role has the Indonesian government played in promoting or suppressing the forest burning?

    5. What is the goal of the Nyaru Menteng Orangutan Rehabilitation Center? Why is the center having difficulty releasing orangutans back into the wild?

    Going Further

    Look at the ingredients lists for processed food items in your kitchen, such as microwave popcorn and peanut butter. How common is palm oil as an ingredient?

    Then read this Times editorial about the environmental downside of palm oil. Do additional research in The Times and elsewhere about the rapidly growing palm oil industry.

    What do you think should be done to make sure that palm oil production doesn’t endanger the environment, including orangutan habitat?
