Paid Notice: Deaths
PASQUALI, JOHN

Published: June 17, 2016

PASQUALI--John, 85, of Palisades Park passed away on June 16, 2016. Beloved husband of 61 years to Rosetta Pasquali (nee Osellame). Devoted father of Mark, and Elizabeth and her spouse John Takakjian. Dear brother of Mary "Aurora" and her husband Alfred Kinn. Loving grandfather of Lauren and Allicyn Takakjian, Christina, Grace, Richard and Angelica Pasquali. Uncle to Albert, Susy, Lucas, Hope, Julia, Christopher, Dorothy, John, Angela and Julie. A United States Veteran, he went on to graduate from St. Peter's College, later completing his MBA at NYU and becoming a CPA. His career began at Price Waterhouse, followed by many years at Young & Rubicam. He was privileged to manage Reagan's campaign finances for the New York metro area. Topping off his long career in finance where he enjoyed 20 years with the New York Football Giants as the CFO and Vice President. In his retirement he enjoyed summers in Rivamonte, Italy. For funeral arrangement information please visit: volkleber.com. 1/3
