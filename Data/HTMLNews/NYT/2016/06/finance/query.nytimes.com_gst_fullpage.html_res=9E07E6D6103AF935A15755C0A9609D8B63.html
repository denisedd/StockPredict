Paid Notice: Deaths
DOYLE, MARGARET BREW

Published: June 26, 2016

DOYLE--Margaret Brew, "Peggy", beloved Matriarch of Babylon, NY, passed on June 21, 2016. Margaret was born in New York City, and was among the first women to graduate in 1948 from the Fordham Business School obtaining a Master's Degree in Finance. She began her career at Whiteweld & Co. on Wall Street. She re-entered the job force when she became a widow and was employed by Grumman Aerospace in the purchasing Department. Margaret retired as Superintendent of North Babylon School District in 1995. She began yet another career at the age of 70 and was a founding principal of Kinetics Mutual funds (The Internet Fund). Margaret was an avid reader of all literature among her favorites were historical novels and biographies. She enjoyed NYC Theater and most cultural events in addition to bridge and crossword puzzles. Margaret traveled the world and for the past 35 years spent the winter months in Rincon, Puerto Rico. She was a friend to many due to her warm nature and her generosity. Margaret was a longtime member of the Babylon Rotary and received the prestigious Paul Harris Award. Margaret had many accomplishments and degrees but she was most proud of her children which she raised singlehandedly and her grandchildren. She stressed Education and would help anybody that needed her assistance. Devoted mother of James G. Doyle, Alexis Doyle Balkonis and husband Ken, Deirdre A. Downs and late husband Philip, Maura E. Doyle, Lawrence P. Doyle and wife Karen, Peter B. Doyle and wife Kathleen Campbell and Monique Doyle Abel and husband Bruce Paul. She was predeceased by her husband James P. Doyle, her brother Richard F. Brew, her son Richard J. Doyle and granddaughter Emily Teed Scott. Cherished grandmother of eight, Caroline Balkonis Usas and her husband Andrew, Richard J. Balkonis, Richard and Katherine Doyle, Kaitlin, Keenan and Alexa Doyle and Brigitte Abel. Margaret had a remarkable life and touched many lives. She will be greatly missed. Reposing at the Fredrick J. Chapey & Sons West Islip Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Hwy. (1/4 mile west of the Robert Moses Cswy.). Celebration of the Liturgy of Christian Burial, Wednesday 9:45am, at St. Joseph's R.C. Church, Babylon, LI. Interment Long Island National Cemetery, Pinelawn, LI. Visiting Tuesday 2:00pm until 4:00pm and 7:00pm until 9:00pm. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the Richard J. Doyle Memorial Fund, C/O West Babylon Alumni, 10 Farmingdale Road, West Babylon, NY 11704. 1/3
