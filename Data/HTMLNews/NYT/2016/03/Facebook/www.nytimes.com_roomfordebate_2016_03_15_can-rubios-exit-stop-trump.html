    Can Rubio’s Exit Stop Trump?

    Introduction

    Republican presidential candidate Sen. Marco Rubio announced he is ending his campaign. Hilary Swift for The New York Times

    The Donald Trump juggernaut left Sen. Marco Rubio on the side of the road in Florida Tuesday, forcing the young senator who once seemed like the likely Republican nominee to suspend his campaign. Who will his supporters vote for now, and will his withdrawal allow the Republican establishment to deny the party’s nomination to Trump?
