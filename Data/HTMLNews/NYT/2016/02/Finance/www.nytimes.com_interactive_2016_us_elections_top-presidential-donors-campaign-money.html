    Million-Dollar Donors in the 2016 Presidential Race

    UPDATED Feb. 9, 2016

    In a stark departure from previous elections, most of the money this cycle is flowing not into the campaigns, but into outside groups like “super PACs” and other political organizations that are allowed to raise unlimited amounts of money from wealthy individuals, labor unions and corporations.
