    Welcome to the Idea Economy

    What Will You Think of Next?

    “We’re now living in an Idea Economy where success is defined by the ability to turn ideas into value faster than your competition.” In this environment, described by HP CEO Meg Whitman, businesses can only succeed if they have both the vision and technological agility to respond to market opportunities and quickly turn ideas into reality.


    Welcome to the Idea Economy

    Each of these short films highlights the innovation behind some of today’s most successful, technology-driven businesses, celebrating the spirit of disruption that inspires us all to think beyond the confines of what’s possible today.

    In a new short film series, “Welcome to the Idea Economy,” award-winning documentarian Alison Klayman teamed up with HP Matter to spotlight companies that are challenging the status quo and disrupting industries with revolutionary thinking and the technology to bring their ideas to life, including: 

    • One Kings Lane – An online design retailer that capitalized on a niche market and became a premier name in ecommerce.

    • Vimeo – A video platform and an online community whose passion for quality has become an industry standard. 

    • Dock to Dish – A seafood program that designed an entirely fresh approach to sustainable food sourcing and turned a movement into marketplace. 


    One Kings Lane: Democratizing the designer retail industry with style

    One Kings Lane didn’t set out to disrupt the home furnishing industry, but its online store of highly curated décor filled a void in a previously limited marketplace.

    Across all industries, a new class of entrepreneurs is changing the rules of the game, forcing businesses to adapt and use technology to rise above their competition.
