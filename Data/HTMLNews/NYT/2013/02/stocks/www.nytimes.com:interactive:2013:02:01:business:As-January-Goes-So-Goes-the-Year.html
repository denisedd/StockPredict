    Off the Charts: As January Goes, So Goes the Year?

    January was a good month for the stock market in the United States, which could be a sign this will be a good year. Only once in the past 63 years did the Standard & Poor’s 500 rise more than 4 percent in January and then end the year lower than it was at the end of January. That was in 1987, which also was the best January in the history of the S. & P. 500.
