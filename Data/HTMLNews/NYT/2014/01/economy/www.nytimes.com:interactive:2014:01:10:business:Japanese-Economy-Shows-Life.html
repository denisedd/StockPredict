    Off the Charts: Japanese Economy Shows Life

    JAN. 10, 2014

    In 2013, the Japanese stock market rose at the fastest rate since 1972 as deflation appeared to end. The economy also grew at the fastest rate in decades. Those changes came as Prime Minister Shinzo Abe and the Bank of Japan sought to stimulate the economy with aggressive fiscal and monetary policies.
