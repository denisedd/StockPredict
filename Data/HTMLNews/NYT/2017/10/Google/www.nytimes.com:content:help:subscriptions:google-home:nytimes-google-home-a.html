  • 2. What is included in the New York Times + Google Home subscription?
    Back to top

    Basic Digital Access subscription

    • Access to NYTimes.com and all NYTimes apps
    • Unlimited article access, anytime, anywhere
    • Discounts to The Times Store, Film Club, Wine Club and TimesTalks

    All Access subscription

    • Access to NYTimes.com and all NYTimes apps
    • Unlimited article access, anytime, anywhere
    • Discounts to The Times Store, Film Club, Wine Club and TimesTalks
    • NYTimes Crossword access
    • NYTimes Cooking access
    • One (1) bonus All Access subscription
    • One (1) Google Home voice-activated speaker powered by Google Assistant (a gift from us while supplies last; shipping charge may apply)

    Home Delivery + All Access subscription

    • Access to NYTimes.com and all NYTimes apps
    • Unlimited article access, anytime, anywhere
    • Discounts to The Times Store, Film Club, Wine Club and TimesTalks
    • NYTimes Crossword access
    • NYTimes Cooking access
    • Home Delivery
    • Exclusive print editions like The New York TImes For Kids
    • Two (2) bonus All Access subscriptions
    • One (1) Google Home voice-activated speaker powered by Google Assistant (a gift from us while supplies last; shipping charge may apply)
  • 3. How much will I be charged for this subscription?
    Back to top
      • Basic Digital Access: For the first year of service, you will be charged in advance by The New York Times a rate of $120.00. After your first year commitment, your subscription to The New York Times Basic Digital Access will automatically renew on a monthly basis and you will be billed in advance at the standard rate of $15.99 every 30 days. Taxes may apply. A Google Home with a retail value of approximately $129 is included (while supplies last; shipping charge may apply).
      • All Access: For the first year of service, you will be charged in advance by The New York Times a rate of $204.00. After your first year commitment, your subscription to The New York Times All Access will automatically renew on a monthly basis and you will be billed at the standard rate of $26.99 every 30 days. Taxes may apply. A Google Home with a retail value of approximately $129 is included (while supplies last; shipping charge may apply).
    • Home Delivery + All Access: For the first year of service, your credit card will be charged the 50% off rate of an annual home delivery subscription in advance (i.e., $234.00) at the time of purchase. After your first year commitment, your subscription to The New York Times Home Delivery + All Access will automatically renew and you will be billed in advance at the standard rate every 28 days. Taxes may apply. Prices are based on Saturday/Sunday delivery in the New York metropolitan area and thus may vary by delivery location and frequency. A Google Home with a retail value of approximately $129 is included (while supplies last; shipping charge may apply).
  • 4. Can I cancel my New York Times + Google Home subscription?
    Back to top
    • When you sign up for a New York Times + Google Home subscription, you are committing to a one year subscription. If you cancel your annual subscription within the first year, the cancellation will not take effect until the end of the year. Your subscription access will continue for the remainder of your one-year subscription and you will not receive a prorated refund.
    • After the one year commitment, you may cancel your subscription at any time by contacting Customer Service at 1-800-698-4637 or via Live Chat and your home delivery and unlimited access will continue for the remainder of the current billing period.
    • Cancellation terms are available at The New York Times Terms of Sale.
  • 5. How do I obtain my Google Home?
    Back to top

    Upon purchase of a qualifying subscription, you will receive a promotional code to use within the Google Store.

    • Subscribers will receive the promo code via email within 7-10 business days of purchase.

    To redeem your promotional code, you can either open the URL within the confirmation email or visit store.google.com, select Buy on the Google Home product page and add the product to your cart. You will need to add your promotional code during checkout. You will receive a notification that the code has been applied and that it will be reflected at checkout.

    During checkout within the Google Store, you will see an estimated delivery date, provided by Google, as well as any shipping fee that may apply as determined by Google.

  • 6. How do I contact customer service?
    Back to top

    Contact The New York Times for any questions regarding your redemption of the promotional code, payment or billing, access to the New York Times content or mobile apps, activating your subscription, questions about your New York Times user ID or password, or cancellation.

    Contact the Google Store team, and mention you are a New York Times subscriber, for any questions regarding your Google device or account, including technical problems, recovering Google Store credentials (username, email address, etc.), changes to your Google Store profile, and Terms of Service for your Google device.
