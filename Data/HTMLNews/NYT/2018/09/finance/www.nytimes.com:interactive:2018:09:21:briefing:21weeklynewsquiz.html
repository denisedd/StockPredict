    News Quiz: Test Your Knowledge of the Week’s Headlines

    By CHRIS STANFORD and ANNA SCHAVERIEN SEPT. 21, 2018

    Did you stay up to date this week? Take our quiz to find out.

    Last week, the question about the women’s champion at the U.S. Open had the highest share of correct answers, with 91 percent. Only 41 percent knew the director of the best film at the Venice International Film Festival.

    The news quiz is published every Friday morning. Click here for other weeks’ quizzes.
