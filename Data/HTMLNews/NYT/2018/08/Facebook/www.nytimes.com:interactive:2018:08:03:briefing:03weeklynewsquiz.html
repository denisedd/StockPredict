    News Quiz: Test Your Knowledge of the Week’s Headlines

    By CHRIS STANFORD and ANNA SCHAVERIEN AUG. 3, 2018

    Did you stay up to date this week? Take our quiz to find out.

    Last week, the question about the country that suffered devastating wildfires had the highest share of correct answers, with 93 percent. Only 42 percent knew the answer to the question about this year’s Kennedy Center Honors.

    The news quiz is published every Friday morning. Click here for other weeks’ quizzes.
