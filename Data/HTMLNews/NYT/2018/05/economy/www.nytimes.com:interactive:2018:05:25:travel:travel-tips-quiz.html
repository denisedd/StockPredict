    This Month's Quiz: How Smart a Traveler Are You?

    Compiled by JUSTIN SABLICH MAY 25, 2018

    Here are a few questions, taken from recent articles in our Travel Tips collection, to test your travel savvy. Related Article

    Despite being in its peak travel season, August is one of the most affordable times to visit Paris.

    True

    False

    The following are ways to enjoy a high-end trip to Los Angeles on a budget:

    Rent a car, rather than relying on ridesharing options like Uber and Lyft.

    Avoid going in summer.

    Avoid cultural attractions like the J. Paul Getty Museum and the Wende Museum, which charge high admission.

    All of the above.

    None of the above.

    You still have time to lock in the most affordable airfare for destinations within the United States.

    True

    False

    Unless you have frequent flier status with an airline, you will likely have to pay to choose the following economy seats:

    Toward the front of the plane, which means you deplane sooner.

    Bulk head seats, which are the front row in economy and usually offer more legroom.

    Exit rows, which have extra legroom.

    None of the above.

    All of the above.

    In general, for the lowest rates, fly on Tuesday or Saturday.

    True

    False

    Visit during the winter.

    Stay in the city center.

    Rely on the subway.

    All of the above.

    If you want to use your smartphone abroad, the most affordable way to do so is to pay your carrier an international roaming fee.
