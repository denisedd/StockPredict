    News Quiz: Test Your Knowledge of the Week’s Headlines

    By CHRIS STANFORD and ANNA SCHAVERIEN MAY 4, 2018

    Did you stay up to date this week? Take our quiz to find out.

    Last week, the question about the shooting in Nashville had the highest share of correct answers, with 95 percent. Not as many of you knew about the Premier League; only 38 percent answered the question about Arsène Wenger correctly.

    The news quiz is published every Friday morning. Click here for other weeks’ quizzes.

    1 of 11

    Revealing a huge archive of stolen nuclear plans, which country accused Iran on Monday of lying for years about its efforts to build a nuclear weapon?

    The accusation came days before President Trump is to decide whether to abandon the 2015 nuclear deal with Iran.

    France

    Israel

    Jordan

    Saudi Arabia

    Signal

    Telegram

    Viber

    Prime Minister Theresa May of Britain suffered a setback this week when the home secretary, Amber Rudd, resigned over her handling of an immigration scandal.

    Where is the Windrush generation originally from?

    Australia

    The Caribbean

    Hong Kong
