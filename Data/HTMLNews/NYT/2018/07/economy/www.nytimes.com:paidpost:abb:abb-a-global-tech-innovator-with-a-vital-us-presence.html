    ABB: A Global Tech Innovator With a Vital U.S. Presence

    ABB is an innovative technology company with more than 130 years of experience and expertise in electrification, digitally driven industrial automation and robotics, and electric-powered transportation. To showcase the power and performance of electric cars, as well as its unrivalled expertise in electrification and leadership in electric vehicle charging solutions, the company is also title sponsor of the international ABB FIA Formula E Championship series, whose season finale races are this month in Brooklyn, N.Y.

    With its world headquarters in Zurich, Switzerland, and 135,000 employees in more than 100 countries, ABB is very much a global corporation. But it is also a major American company, with more than 20,000 engineers, technology experts and other employees in 100 communities across the country.
